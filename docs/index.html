Smart Tournament Management

SportDeck is a full-featured, role-based tournament platform for admins, players, and fans. Schedule matches, manage teams, track results — all in one place.

Get Started → View Database Schema

What's Included

Everything you need to run a sports league out of the box.

🔐

Role-Based Auth

Separate portals for Admins and Players with session-based login and password hashing.

🏅

Tournament Management

Create multi-sport tournaments with statuses: upcoming, ongoing, and completed. Set player caps.

👥

Team & Player Roster

Manage teams and their rosters. Link registered users to player records within tournaments.

📅

Match Scheduling

Schedule matches between any two teams with date, time, and venue. Update status after completion.

📊

Results & Fixtures

Record scores, declare winners, and publicly display fixtures and results — no login required to view.

📝

Self Registration

Players can browse open tournaments and register themselves. Admins can approve or cancel registrations.

Two Portals, One System

SportDeck serves both administrators and players from the same installation.
